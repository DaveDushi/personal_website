About Me

My name is David Dusi, I'm 23 years old and currently studying Computer Science in Reichman University. I was born and raised as an Orthodox Jew in the Brooklyn Syrian Community. At the age of 18 I came to Israel as part of a gap year program and studied in Yeshivat Birkat Moshe. From the second I stepped foot in Israel I fell in love with the people and culture and decided to stay. I joined the Hesder Army program in my yeshiva and drafted to the IDF in August 2019. I served in the Combat Engineering unit for 20 months. After my service I decided I want to study Computer Science and chose Reichman University's program.
